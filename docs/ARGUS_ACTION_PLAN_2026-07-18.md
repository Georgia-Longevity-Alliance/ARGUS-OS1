# ARGUS-OS1 — Action Plan, July 2026

## 1. Pilot: Centrin1-GFP SNR — 4 weeks, ~$1,650

**Goal:** Prove Centrin1-GFP signal from centrioles is detectable at minimal LED power (SNR ≥ 5 at ≤5% LED, ≤200 ms exposure) on a 40×/0.95 dry objective.

**Before spending money:** Ask on the OpenFlexure forum if anyone has a Centrin1-GFP RPE1 or HeLa line they can share. Many labs have these sitting in the freezer.

**If no free line is available:**
- RPE1-hTERT: ATCC CRL-4000, ~$400
- Centrin1-GFP plasmid: Addgene #26757 or similar, ~$100
- Lentivirus packaging: commercial service, ~$800
- FACS sorting: university core facility, ~$200

**Minimal version (free if you have microscope access):**
- Borrow any GFP-labeled cell line from a friendly lab
- Use any widefield fluorescence microscope with a 40×/0.95 objective
- Take 50 frames at different LED powers
- Measure SNR with ImageJ

**Go/No-Go:** SNR ≥ 5 at ≤5% LED → proceed. SNR < 5 → reconsider optical design.
